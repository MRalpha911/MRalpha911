About Us

Metrologyx Training and Assessment Center Inc. (MeTACI) is a Techinical Vocational Institution (TechVoc) duly registered under the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and accredited with TESDA. We specialize in training programs in the field of industrial Automation and Control.
Metrologyx is to arm students when commencing work as Instrumentation, control & automation technician or engineer are missing from the engineering programs. Here at Metrologyx, we develop training programs to bridge the gap between education and industry requirements in the areas of process Instrumentation, Control & Automation.

Why Choose Us?

Cebu Metrologyx Technical Sercives (CMTS) provides calibration service for industrial intruments such as temperature controllers and pressure transmitters, pressure gauges as well as electrical measuring instruments. CMTS operates in 4F Copenhagen East Bldg. A.C. Cortes Ave, Cambaro, Mandaue City, Cebu.

Cebu Metrologyx Techinical Services (CMTS) Calibration Laboratory is organized to operate in accordance with the requirements of PNS ISO/IEC 1702:2017, whether carrying out work in its permanent facilities or at onsite locations.
